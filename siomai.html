About Us

Siomai King is a proudly Filipino food business known for serving delicious, high-quality, and affordable siomai. Established to bring the authentic taste of siomai to every Filipino, Siomai King has become a trusted name in the food industry.

Our mission is to provide flavorful siomai made from the freshest ingredients, while helping aspiring entrepreneurs achieve success through our franchising opportunities. Siomai King believes in empowering Filipinos to grow their own businesses and share the royal taste with everyone.

At Siomai King, we serve happiness, opportunity, and the Royalty in Every Bite!
